# Smello Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2025-01-01**

Smello is committed to meeting all App Store Review Guidelines, especially Section 5.1 regarding data collection and user privacy. This document explains how Smello collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

---

# 1. Summary of Data Collection

Smello does **NOT**:
- Collect personal identifiable information (PII)
- Track users across apps or websites
- Collect precise location
- Access camera, microphone, contacts, or other sensitive data
- Collect advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- Upload personal data to a server
- Use third‑party advertising SDKs
- Perform profiling or analytics for marketing

Smello operates entirely on-device except for optional update checks or feedback submissions.

---

# 2. Information We Collect

## 2.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily (Optional)
- Avatar (stored locally)
- Nickname (stored locally)
- Training records: dates, training sets, intensity ratings
→ *Used solely for generating progress charts and training logs.*

All data stays **on your device**.

---

## 2.2 Information Collected Automatically (For Stability Only)
Smello may collect:
- Anonymous crash logs (no personal identifiers)
- Device model, OS version, region settings
- App runtime states (errors, crashes)

Used strictly for improving app stability.

---

# 3. Permission Usage

| Permission | Purpose | Required | Data uploaded? |
|------------|----------|----------|----------------|
| **Notifications** | Reminders for training | Optional | No |
| **Photo Library** | Avatar upload, export charts | Optional | No |
| **Network Access** | Update check, feedback | Required (offline mode works) | No |

Smello does **NOT** request:
Camera, microphone, contacts, health data, motion data, Bluetooth, location.

---

# 4. How We Use Your Data

Smello uses your data exclusively for:
- Displaying and analyzing your training progress
- Reminding you about training (if notifications enabled)
- Improving stability & fixing bugs

Smello does **NOT**:
- Show ads
- Share any data with third parties
- Use analytics for profiling
- Track you across apps

---

# 5. Data Storage & Deletion

- All data is stored entirely **on your device**.
- Deleting the app will remove all stored data.
- You may manually delete training logs at any time.

No cloud storage or server synchronization is used.

---

# 6. Child Privacy

Smello is safe for general audiences.
Minors should use the app with guardian consent.

---

# 7. Policy Updates

If future versions introduce cloud backup or new data usage, we will notify you and request explicit user consent.
